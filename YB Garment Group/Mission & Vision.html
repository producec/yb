YB Introduction

YB Garment Group is one of the best garment producers in China. Our group has more than 10 factories with best quality all over China, and we has more than 20 years experiences in producing garments. By now, our group has approved by WRAP (WORLDWIDE RESPONSIBLE ACCREDITED PRODUCTION), and Interek. Based on these approval, YB Garment Group are capable to assure to produce high quality products and cooperate with more international brands, such as DKNY, PUMA and etc. The main factory of our group has more than 200 machines and around 250 experienced workers and officers, thus, the monthly productivity can be reached 200,000 units. With all the productivity of the joint factories in our group, the monthly productivity can be reached 2 million units.

Our Mission

YB Garment Group produces women's clothing, men's clothing, kids' clothing, handbags, and new technical material garments. Our professional staff will provide you your required photo sample. We patiently hear your needs, and offer good services and products. Buyers are also welcome to visit our show room. Through our experienced factories, you will get your products with high quality on time at reasonable prices. To provide you with satisfying services is always our goal.
